III · Platform

Shared roots.

05 Core
Pillars

Every Drasil Nova app is built on the same bedrock — because good infrastructure shouldn't be rebuilt from scratch each time.

F.01 · Privacy

POPIA-compliant, by design.

We follow South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act — not as an afterthought, but baked in. Your data stays in South Africa. No grey areas.

F.02 · Offline-First

The signal returns. The work catches up.

Load-shedding is a fact of life. Our apps cache everything locally and sync when connectivity returns. No half-saved forms. No lost data. Built for stage 6, stage 8, and everything in between.

F.03 · Nova ID

One login, every app.

Sign in once across all Drasil Nova products. No new password per app.

F.04 · Pricing

Rand prices. Always.

No dollar conversions. No surprise billing when the rand dips. What you see is what you pay — in rands, via PayFast.

F.05 · Open Data

Your data is yours.

Export everything, anytime. CSV, JSON, PDF. We don't hold your data hostage.

F.06 · Roadmap

Two new stars, currently forming.

EduGate (screen-time gating for children) and the Worx Suite (invoicing for SA tradespeople) are in active development. Follow the build in public.
