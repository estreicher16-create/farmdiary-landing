What the Livestock App does

Your whole herd.
Always under control.

Built for South African conditions — works offline, handles SA breed names, and tells you when cows have skipped calving.

🐄
Animal Register
Track every animal — cattle, goats, sheep, pigs, game and more. Tag numbers, breeds, birth dates, groups and paddocks. Full history on every animal.
🍼
Calf Register & Calving %
Log every birth with mother tag, sex and date. Automatically calculates your calving percentage and alerts when cows have skipped calving cycles.
🚨
Health & Sick List
Log sick and injured animals instantly from your phone. Track what's wrong, which paddock they're in, and when they recovered.
💊
Bulk Treatments
Record dipping, vaccinations and medicine with dosage and cost per animal. Select multiple animals at once. Full treatment history per animal.
👷
Worker Management
Workers clock in and out with a PIN on their own phone. Track attendance, hours worked, and who logged what on the farm.
📄
PDF & Excel Reports
Download professional reports for your bank, vet or insurance. Export full registers to Excel for your accountant. One tap — done.
🏕️
Groups & Paddocks
Organise your farm into groups and paddocks. See which animals are where at a glance. Move animals between paddocks in one tap.
🔒
Secure Login NEW
Email & password login with email verification. Your farm data is private and secure — only you can see your animals and records.
☁️
Cloud Backup
Your data is saved to the cloud automatically. Log something on your phone in the veld — it's on your PC when you get home.
🐔 Poultry App — farm-dairy-poultry.vercel.app
What the Poultry App does

Every flock.
Every egg.
Every hatch.

Purpose-built for poultry farmers — broilers, layers, ducks, turkeys, ostriches, geese and pigeons. Not a generic livestock tool.

🐔
Flock Register NEW
Add and manage flocks for 7 poultry types. Track birds in, birds alive, breed, supplier, house, cost per bird and intake date. Full flock history.
📊
Daily Production Logs
Log daily egg count, mortalities, culled birds, feed and water per flock. Automatic lay percentage and mortality tracking over time.
🐣
Breeder Cages NEW
Manage individual breeder cages with hen and rooster counts. Log eggs per cage daily, track feed and water, and ring individual birds with full profiles.
🥚
Incubation Manager NEW
Set incubation batches for any poultry type — each species has its own incubation period, candle days and lockdown schedule auto-calculated.
🕯️
Candle Check Logging NEW
Log fertile, clear, blood ring and cracked eggs per candle check. Instantly see fertility %, how many to remove, and estimated hatch count.
📋
Incubation Reference Guide NEW
Built-in notice board showing correct temperature, humidity, turning schedule and lockdown day for each species — chicken to ostrich.
💰
P&L per Flock
Track sales of birds, eggs and chicks. See running profit or loss per flock with ROI percentage, revenue breakdown and estimated feed costs.
💊
Treatment & Vaccination Tracker
Log all treatments, vaccinations and vitamins per flock. See vaccination schedule per species and tick off completed treatments.
✏️
Edit All Records NEW
Edit any flock, cage, bird or daily log record after the fact. Correct mistakes without starting over — changes sync to the cloud instantly.
